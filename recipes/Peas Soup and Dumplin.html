Peas Soup and Dumpling

What is Peas Soup and Dumpling?

The orignal soup of The Bahamas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ham, diced
  • 8 cups Water
  • ½ lb Salt beef, diced
  • 2 Onions, chopped
  • 2 Celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 Bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 Pigeon peas can
  • 2 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 3 Thyme sprigs
  • 3 Corn, chopped
  • 2 Sweet potato, chopped
  • 2 cups Pumpkin, chopped
  • 1 Cassava, peeled & chopped
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp Pepper sauce (optional)

    • Dumplings

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ cup Water

Bring it together!!

  1. Place ham and other meats in separate pots. Do not add salt to ham or salt beef. Allow to boil until fork tender.
  2. Boil and discard salt beef broth repeat 3 times.
  3. Strain and save the broth from the ham. Put on the side. Cut the meat into bite size pieces.
  4. In a large pot, heat oil add the onions, celery, and thyme. Sauté for 10 minutes, then add tomato paste, stir frequently to avoid the tomato paste from burning and cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Add cassava, pumpkin, sweet potato and corn.
  6. Add peas and the broth from the ham and allow to boil then bring to a simmer by turning down the heat. Allow to simmer for 1/2 hour. Adjust seasoning if needed with salt and Grandma A's pepper sauce (optional). While simmering make the dumplings.
  7. Stir the flour, and salt together, then stir in water until a dough forms.
  8. Cut the dough into small pieces. Form the dough by rolling between both hands until it forms into thick strips and drop into the simmering soup.
  9. Let cook for 10 minutes, then cover and cook another 10 minutes. Serve.

Enjoy a taste of The Bahamas
