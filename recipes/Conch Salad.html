Conch Salad

What is Conch Salad?

Conch filled Ceviche native to The Bahamas.

Ingredients

  • "Most conch salads are comprised of bell pepper, onion, tomato, raw conch, citrus fruits and local spices. The spices and citrus contrast one another in a way that allows both flavors to stand out, and yet they also blend to a point that neither feels out of place. The delicious juices of the citrus fruit also create the perfect addition to the juicy conch meat."Bahamas Conch Salad

Bring it together!!

  1. Place all diced vegtebles into bowl
  2. Add raw diced conch meat into bowl
  3. Squeeze lime and orange into bowl
  4. Add a pinch of salt
  5. Mix all ingredients together
  6. Add habanero pepper to taste

Enjoy a taste of The Bahamas
